Water and energy supply and consumption (WESC)

We are developing an appropriate information model, called the Water and energy supply and consumption Markup Language (WESCML), for representing the information for the water and energy supply and consumption datasets for this project, which will be used to generate XML schemas for encoding the data. The information model allows for data to be delivered in a consistent format. The diagrams below depict the draft version of the information model.
